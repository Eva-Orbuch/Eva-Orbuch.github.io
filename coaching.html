Individual Life and Leadership Coaching and Organizational Development Coaching

I work in a holistic way with individuals who are deeply committed to their lives and want support to grow in any or all of the following areas:

Personal organization and time management

  • Your personal leadership journey
  • New managers in a workplace looking to be effective managers and run great teams
  • Creative goals (both the creative process and the business side of being an artist)
  • Finding your authentic activism, contributions to community, and an enriching political life
  • Connecting to your spiritual journey and ancestral/spiritual lineage

I also coach organizations, groups, or teams to assess their circumstances and what their goals for change are. I offer coaching, and plan and facilitate retreats or important meetings and group processes.
